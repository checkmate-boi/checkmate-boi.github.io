From a young age Conner Colquhoun had an interest in the game of Chess. Having a natural talent for it, he quickly climbs the ranks of the game of strategy. But with the weight of being at the summit looming above him, will his love for the game live on?

ABOUT

This story was inspired by and is based around the life of Conner Colquhoun, better known as, the 93%.

On episode 6 of the Trash Taste Podcast, Conner revealed that he had been professionally involved with chess when he was younger, in a very anime-esque story. Joey made a joke about how he'd love to see a manga adaptation of it, and I immediately started mapping out a long-term story about it. And after getting my artist friend to agree to help with the art, we officially announced "Checkmate".
